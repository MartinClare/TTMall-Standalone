Your GitHub Repo: https://github.com/MartinClare/TTMall-Standalone
Status: ✅ Code pushed and ready!

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

STEP 1: Go to Railway
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🌐 Open: https://railway.app


STEP 2: Sign In with GitHub
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. Click "Login" → "Login with GitHub"
2. Authorize Railway
3. You'll see your dashboard


STEP 3: Deploy from GitHub
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. Click "New Project"
2. Choose "Deploy from GitHub repo"
3. Select: TTMall-Standalone
4. Wait 2-3 minutes for build ⏱️


STEP 4: Add Volume (IMPORTANT!)
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. Click your service
2. Go to "Settings" tab
3. Find "Volumes" section
4. Click "+ New Volume"
5. Mount path: /app/public/videos
6. Size: 1 GB (or larger if needed)
7. Click "Add"

⚠️ Without this, uploaded videos will disappear on redeploy!
📝 Videos are stored in: public/videos/


STEP 5: Get Your URL
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
1. Go to "Settings" tab
2. Find "Domains" section
3. Click "Generate Domain"
4. Copy your URL (looks like):
   https://ttmall-standalone-production.up.railway.app
5. VISIT YOUR LIVE APP! 🎉


━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✅ THAT'S IT! Your app is LIVE!

Test it:
- Browse videos
- Upload a video file OR add a public URL
- Edit video info (title, stats, live status)
- Test shopping cart
- Videos persist across restarts
- Share the URL with friends!
